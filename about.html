UNESWA ENGINEERING SOCIETY STRATEGIC DIRECTION

The document outlines the strategic direction of the UNESWA ENGINEERING SOCIETY, which in a nutshell, are foundational ideas and actions that will guide the non-profit organization towards achieving its long-term aspiration.



Vision

To be a driving force in producing engineers who are productive and socially responsible.


Mission

1. Empower: To equip engineering students in Uneswa with essential knowledge through field trips, seminars, projects, and masterclasses, while also establishing mentorship programs and peer tutoring to drive academic excellence and inspire future engineers.

2.Connect: To create a supportive and inclusive social network through events and activities that bring students together, fostering a sense of community and belonging.

3.Engage: To inform, engage, and inspire engineering students in Uneswa, cultivating a passion for the field and empowering active involvement in the society's initiatives.

4.Cultivate: To participate in community outreach programs, providing students with opportunities to develop presentation, communication skills, and inspire the next generation of engineers in both university and high school students.


Values

1. Academic Excellence: Striving for the highest standards of academic achievement and fostering an environment where students support each other's learning and growth.

2. Mentorship: Promoting the value of mentorship and peer tutoring to inspire and guide both university and high school students in their pursuit of engineering.

3. Community Engagement: Embracing the responsibility to give back to the community and encouraging active participation in outreach programs.

4. Innovation and Learning: Fostering a culture of continuous learning, curiosity, and innovation within the field of electrical and electronic engineering.

5. Inclusivity and Diversity: Celebrating and respecting the diversity of backgrounds, experiences, and perspectives within the engineering community.

6. Integrity: Promoting honesty, ethical behaviour, and responsibility in all aspects of academic and professional life.

7. Commitment and discipline: Unwavering dedication to achieving goals and adhering to principles, even in the face of challenges or distractions.
