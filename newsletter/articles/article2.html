AI in Modern Engineering TRENDING

Artificial Intelligence is revolutionizing engineering practices across multiple disciplines, from structural design to process optimization.

At UNESWA, engineers are using AI to predict structural failures before they occur, analyzing data from sensors embedded in buildings and bridges. This predictive maintenance approach has reduced costs by 30% in pilot projects and increased safety standards.

Furthermore, AI-driven robotics are being implemented in manufacturing, allowing for precision engineering that surpasses human capabilities in repetitive tasks.
