Strategic Direction

The document outlines the strategic direction of the UNESWA ENGINEERING SOCIETY, which in a nutshell, are foundational ideas and actions that will guide the non-profit organization towards achieving its long-term aspiration.

Vision

To be a driving force in producing engineers who are productive and socially responsible.

Mission

1. Empower: Equip engineering students with essential knowledge through field trips, seminars, projects, and masterclasses.
2. Connect: Create a supportive social network through events and activities.
3. Engage: Inform, engage, and inspire students to cultivate a passion for engineering.
4. Cultivate: Participate in community outreach to inspire the next generation.

Values

Academic Excellence | Mentorship | Community Engagement | Innovation | Inclusivity | Integrity | Commitment
